Episode 2: The Darkness Falls
Without warning, the ground began to tremble. A low, rumbling sound crept through the earth, growing louder by the second. The sky—once blue and comforting—darkened unnaturally. People looked up in horror as the heavens turned pitch black, the stars vanishing in an instant. Strange white objects appeared, dotting the void above. At first, they seemed distant, like tiny flecks of dust. But then, they multiplied, growing larger, moving closer. Panic rippled through the streets as the mysterious objects descended toward Earth, leaving a sense of dread in their wake.
